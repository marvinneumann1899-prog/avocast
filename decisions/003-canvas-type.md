# 003 — Canvas type: Lean Canvas

**Date:** 2026-04-28
**Status:** Accepted

## Context

The brief requires "a Canvas model" but does not specify which one. Three relevant options:
1. **Lean Canvas** (Ash Maurya, 9 boxes)
2. **Business Model Canvas** (Osterwalder, 9 boxes)
3. **Value Proposition Canvas** (Osterwalder, 2 sides)

The IU course book "Introduction to Academic Work" (DLBCSIAW01) does not cover Canvas tools — verified via full-text search of the PDF.

## Decision

**Lean Canvas.**

## Rationale

- The brief's three explicit requirements — "key problem", "users", "value proposition" — map 1:1 to Lean Canvas boxes 1, 2, and 3. Business Model Canvas has no dedicated "Problem" box.
- Lean Canvas is purpose-built for **early-stage product ideas / prototypes**, which AvoCast is.
- Compact and concrete: 9 boxes, each filled with a few bullets. Avoids the bloat of a full BMC analysis.
