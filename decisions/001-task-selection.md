# 001 — Task 2 (AvoCast) chosen over Task 1 (Yelp NLP)

**Date:** 2026-04-28
**Status:** Accepted

## Context

The course brief offers two task options:
- Task 1 — Market-Insight Bot (Yelp reviews + NLP / sentiment analysis)
- Task 2 — AvoCast (US avocado prices + Facebook Prophet forecasting)

## Decision

Task 2 — AvoCast.

## Rationale

Marvin's preference. Time-series forecasting with Prophet is more aligned with the AI in Business program direction than NLP sentiment analysis on Yelp data. Avocado pricing is a clean, well-documented dataset and the business translation (retailers, wholesalers, growers) is concrete.
